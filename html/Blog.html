How Technology Has Affected Our Daily Life

Technology has affected completely changed our lives, in a positive or in a negative way. How we go on with our day to day activities has completely changed. Adults, teenagers and even kids carry around a smart phone with them everywhere they go. The first thing we do as right after waking up is probably checking our phones to either turn off our alarms or check our social media applications. Advancement of technology resulted in alot of positive and negative changes. It is up to the person and how they utilize it.
According to global tech care company Asurion, people check their smartphones 96 times a day. That's once every 10 minutes. This over dependance is bascially a form of addiction. The constant usage of smartphones also shortens our attention span. Many people take technology for granted. Technology has enabled us to communicate with people across the world. Thanks to COVID-19 and the lockdown, even schools and colleges take their classes thorugh the help of video calling. One can basically buy all their basic needs while sitting in their room through online shopping. You basically have everything in the palm of your hands. It depends on how one chooses to use it.
To summarise, Technology advancements has shown a substantial growth in every field. Science and technology is incredibly important for progress, advancement and change.
